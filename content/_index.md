I'm currently offering the following services:

* __Systems programming__. Building from first principles is my bread and butter. Past consulting engagements include [researching interplanetary IP communication]({{< ref "/blog/2025-quic-in-space.md" >}}), [developing a dependency solver for the Conda ecosystem]({{< ref "/blog/2023-magic-dependency-resolution.md" >}}) and [creating a continuous benchmarking setup for rustls]({{< ref "/blog/2024-continuous-benchmarking-rustls.md" >}}). I also led the design and implementation of a container image builder, registry and runtime in collaboration with [Outerbounds](https://outerbounds.com) (see [their article](https://outerbounds.com/blog/faster-cloud-compute) for details, or my own [related]({{< ref "/blog/2023-crafting-oci-images.md" >}}) [write-ups]({{< ref "/blog/2024-whale-in-a-bucket.md" >}})).
* __Boosting Rust open source projects__. I've been [contributing to the Rust ecosystem since 2014]({{< ref "/blog/2022-how-i-got-involved-in-rust.md" >}}). I have experience designing, implementing and polishing features in collaboration with project maintainers. As one example, check out [this article]({{< ref "/blog/2024-continuous-benchmarking-rustls.md" >}}) about a project for [ISRG](https://www.abetterinternet.org/) (makers of [Let's Encrypt](https://letsencrypt.org/)). Other past clients were [Prefix.dev](https://prefix.dev/), [Stormshield](https://www.stormshield.com/) and the [Solana Foundation](https://solana.org/).
* __Free-form consulting__. If there's anything else you feel I could help with, let's talk and find out whether my skills could bring you closer to a solution. Even if I cannot help you directly, there's a good chance I know someone who can.

#### Ways of working together

* __Solo__. I am a _very_ intrinsically motivated person, and have a proven track record delivering high-quality results with little guidance. I proactively ask the necessary questions to clarify assumptions and goals, and I research my way forward through any obstacles that might appear. I am also able to summarize and explain a project's progress in such a way that you can follow along without being burdened with day-to-day minutiae.
* __Team__. Peers see me as a highly talented and pleasant team member (see [testimonials](#testimonials) above). My technical skills are accompanied by the capacity to effectively collaborate with others: I feel comfortable mentoring junior programmers, solving architectural challenges with experienced developers, interfacing with business stakeholders, and more. If you are looking to boost your team, I'd be happy to be on board.

#### Practicalities

* __Location__. I am currently based in Santiago (Chile) and have a preference for remote work.
* __Working across time zones__. I have experience collaborating with colleagues in different time zones (mainly US and Europe).
* __Time commitment__. I am usually interested in projects for 1 to 3 days a week, 8 hours a day.
* __Languages__. I am fully proficient in English, Dutch and Spanish.
